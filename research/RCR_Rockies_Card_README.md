# RCR Rockies Card — Resort List

**Official info & purchase:** https://skircr.com/purchase/rcr-rockies-card/

The **RCR Rockies Card** is a discount / loyalty card offered by Resorts of the Canadian Rockies (RCR). It typically provides:
- A few **free days** (e.g., 3 free days) at participating resorts
- Ongoing **discounts** on additional days
- Region-specific perks and restrictions

Always review the official page for the current season’s exact terms.

---

## Participating Resorts (RCR Rockies Card)

The card is valid at the following RCR resorts in Western Canada:

- Fernie Alpine Resort — British Columbia, Canada
- Kicking Horse Mountain Resort — British Columbia, Canada
- Kimberley Alpine Resort — British Columbia, Canada
- Nakiska Ski Area — Alberta, Canada

---

## Notes

- RCR sometimes runs additional reciprocal or partner deals that are separate from the Rockies Card itself.
- This README treats the Rockies Card as a “multi-resort pass” for app/planning purposes, even though it functions more like a discounted punch card than an unlimited season pass.
