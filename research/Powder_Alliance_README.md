# Powder Alliance — Resort List

**Official info & resort map:** https://www.powderalliance.com/

> ⚠️ Powder Alliance is a reciprocal partner network. You must hold a qualifying season pass at a participating resort to get 3 free days at *each* partner mountain. Participating resorts can change; always confirm details on the official site and with your home mountain before you buy.

## How the Powder Alliance Works (high level)

- Buy a **qualifying season pass** at any Powder Alliance resort.
- Get **3 free days** at *each* of the other Alliance resorts (blackout dates and restrictions can apply).
- Access spans multiple U.S. states plus Canada, Japan, and New Zealand, depending on season.

## Practical Notes

- The Powder Alliance has evolved over the years (resorts added/removed, some shifting to other partnerships or ownership structures).
- Always verify:
  - That your **home resort pass** qualifies for Powder Alliance benefits.
  - The **exact list of partner resorts** and any blackout dates.
  - Whether there are **capacity limits** or pre-registration requirements at specific areas.
